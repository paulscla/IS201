About

A certified life coach, I am a wellness professional who helps people make progress in their lives, through decision making, thought processes, and aid in reaching greater potential and achieving overall fulfillment.

Life Coach & Wellness Professional

Less than the intensity of a therapist, more trusted in advice than a family member, as willing as a friend to listen without any judgment; my goal is to ask the right questions to help analyze your mind and decisions, sit with uncomfortable feelings and truths, hold you accountable in becoming a better version of yourself and provide encouragement to push you in your day-to-day life.

I offer encouragement in recovery from any form of disordered eating (to trademark this term, because we all in some way, shape, or form, hold distorted and deceptive beliefs, opinions, and ideas based around food and how/what we eat) as well as for diagnosed, self or other, eating disorders. My position is to hold you accountable in your journey into and with recovery, to communicate effectively alongside your current thoughts and situations, and to get to the heart of your specific needs and desires.

Skills

I am certified through the Life Coach School and experienced with both dialectical and cognitive behavioral therapy. I specialize in ED recovery as well as help with the day-to-day mental obstacles that we all face. I am here to add perspective on your mind and your life, so we can work through emotions and thoughts and begin to create results.
